This year’s Spring and Summer Graduate Exhibition at the Maryland Institute College of Art will present work to the MICA community and the public from January–July, 2014. The season includes Fine Art and Graphic Design exhibitions and critiques, gallery talks, installations, symposia, public programs, and student curated shows throughout the city of Baltimore.
